Chapter One

Bennett

9:00 am — 120 Lars Street
scroll

I was already awake before the alarm went off.

I don't know how long I'd been staring at the ceiling. Long enough.

Morning goes per usual. Same routine, same breakfast, same everything.

My class starts at 9:30, so I need to rush.

— Opens Mailbox —

Bills, flyers, and an envelope I've never seen before.

It's from the hospital — for dad. He never gets stuff like this by mail. It isn't even sealed.

I don't know what this is, but I take it inside anyway.

— Opens Mail —

What's this..?

Dad's gone to work. My brother's in the living room. I have maybe four minutes before I should leave.

I'm really curious. I open it.

It's a patient file. Clinical, formatted, dense. The kind of thing clearly not meant for me.

I almost put it back.

Then a line stops me.

I read it again.

Last name: Bennett.

I read it a third time.

Date of birth: 14/03/2002.

The first name is one I don't recognize. But everything else.

I set it down. Pick it up again.

This person has my last name. My exact birthday. And it's issued to dad.

Who is this?

Chapter Two
Coincidence

I stare at myself and try to think straight.

It's just a coincidence.

Same last name — it's not that rare. Same birthday — one in 365, that's not impossible.

But the same last name and the same birthday.

At a hospital called Clearview. In a letter sent to my dad, who has worked there for years and has never once mentioned a patient. Never the type to talk.

I was about to call my dad.

Until I turn the letter over.

Shit.

I wasn't supposed to see this.
